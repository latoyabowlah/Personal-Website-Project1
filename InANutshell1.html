My name is LaToya Bowlah and I am a burgeoning marketing and public relations strategist who is passionate about creative solutions that increase meaningful connections between brands and their audience.

With an innate sense of creativity and desire to develop compelling stories coupled with an understanding and admiration for the changing landscape of marketing, I am an innovative communicator who is continuously exploring new strategies and taking on challenges.

Prior to my current role, I began pursuing my career during my Junior year of college through consecutive internship opportunities with Comcast, MSNBC. Here I gained a hands-on understanding of all aspects of media production from segment producing and guest booking, to developing video teasers and digital content. During the Summer of my Senior year at Baruch I began writing for Viacom’s BET Networks. I not only produced timely aggregated news stories across all the publication’s sections but I also wrote and published original content. Later in 2014 I was tapped for a thought leadership opportunity to write op-eds responding to current cultural and lifestyle issues relevant to Millennial women. My journalism foundation has since served as an asset in my marketing and PR projects today.
